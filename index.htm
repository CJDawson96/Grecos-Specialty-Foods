We are an authentic European specialty foods store located in the heart of Surrey, BC, offering an array of products from around the world since our opening in 1984.

The Greco's Family

We take immense joy in the timeless art of providing specialty products that epitomize simplicity and deliciousness. Our passion lies in the curation of artesianal charcuterie and other gourmet goods from around the world.

For those seeking culinary adventure and the allure of new flavours, we invite you to embark on an unforgettable journey to our location nestled in Surrey, BC. In the spirit of savouring exceptional food, we encourage you to take a moment and embrace a moment of relaxation.
